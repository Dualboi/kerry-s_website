Publishing Services Assistant — IOP Publishing

Provides editorial and peer review support across journals.

The Publishing Services Assistant provides a high-quality and efficient peer review service and support for authors, reviewers and in-house publishing and production staff as articles move through the publication process.

The role also ensures that customer and journal data is accurate, complete and kept up to date at all times, supporting smooth publication workflows and a positive experience for all stakeholders.

Responsibilities
  • Processing revised submissions and acceptances through the online submission system.
  • Responding to customer queries in a timely fashion via shared mailboxes.
  • Providing a high-quality, professional service to authors, reviewers and in-house publishing and production staff.
  • Providing cover on other journals where necessary, as instructed by the Managing Editor.
  • Keeping the team informed of any problems with systems or issues that may affect workflows.
  • Following agreed best-practice procedures to ensure timely and efficient movement of articles through the publication process, minimising receipt-to-first-decision and acceptance-to-publication times.
  • Undertaking continuous improvement initiatives to increase peer review efficiency and quality.
  • Ensuring that all required permissions and copyright agreements are received and accurate prior to acceptance.
Key Focus Areas / KPIs
  • Meeting established turnaround times for revised submissions and post-acceptance checks.
  • Replying to all mailbox queries within one working day.
  • Maintaining efficient, professional communication and positive customer feedback.

Skills / Tools: Microsoft Word, Excel and email systems; database and submission system use; strong written and verbal communication; excellent organisation, time and task management; attention to detail; team working. Competencies include customer focus, drive for results, perseverance, planning, time management and written communication.
