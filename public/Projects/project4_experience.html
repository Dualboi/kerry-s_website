Publishing Assistant

Publishing Assistant — Ringwood Publishing

Glasgow (Remote) • Jan 2026 — Present

I remotely volunteer at Ringwood Publishing in Edinburgh, an independent Scottish publisher whose list focuses on Scottish themes in politics, crime, football and culture, featuring authors who are Scottish or resident in Scotland.

I currently proofread manuscripts from authors such as Carol Fox and J.S. Apsley against Ringwood’s standards and practices and create detailed reader’s reports, which are fed back to the commissioning editors and text editors.

Responsibilities
  • Proofreading manuscripts in line with Ringwood’s house style, standards and practices.
  • Producing reader’s reports for commissioning editors and text editors.
  • Supporting authors through the editorial and publishing process.
  • Contributing copy and ideas for the Ringwood Literary Newsletter, Ringwood Podcast (series 6) and YouTube channel videos.
  • Supporting social media promotion and marketing of books.
  • Assisting with the organisation of literary launches and events.
Key Achievements
  • Developed practical experience across the publishing workflow, from manuscript assessment to marketing and events.
  • Consistently delivered clear, actionable editorial feedback that supports commissioning and text editing decisions.

Skills / Tools: editing, proofreading, copyediting, author support, social media promotion and marketing, event organisation, newsletter and podcast content creation.
