My TikTok

I run the TikTok account @bookedbykerry, which started as a passion project to share book reviews, recommendations and engage in booktok trends turned into something really special.

Over the last year, my content has gained 239k views, 12.5k likes, 5k profile views and 927 comments! But most importantly, I have built a community of like-minded, book-loving people, who share and express their ideas and enthusiasm for reading.

I am now part of TikTok's creator affiliate marketing programme, working with publishers such as Hachette UK, HarperCollins and Bloomsbury via their creator mailing lists to create content to promote their books for a commission. I am also part of Bloomsbury's Exclusive Creator Circle which sends proofs, new books and invites to book events monthly. I also work with other independent bookish brands such as Penny Post to create content for commission.
