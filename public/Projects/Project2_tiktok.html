My TikTok project

I run the TikTok account @bookedbykerry, which started as a passion project to express my love of books and reading. It developed into a platform where I now earn commission promoting books and bookish items I love. I have been approached by small bookish brands such as Penny Post to create content for commission. I am also part of the HarperCollins TikTok Creator mailing list to request new editions exclusive to HarperCollins for promotion on TikTok Shop. When I hit 3,000 followers in the new year, I will join the Bloomsbury Exclusive Creator Circle which sends proofs, new books, and invites to book events monthly.
