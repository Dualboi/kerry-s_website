Hey, I’m Kerry! Welcome to my online portfolio where you’ll find everything I have to offer: education, work experience, writing, certifications, graphic designs and more. This is a digital scrapbook of my transferable skills to book publishing and marketing.

I'm an enthusiastic English Literature graduate, publishing services volunteer and website administrator ready to join the publishing industry. Since graduating from university, I have committed myself to learning about the publishing world through courses, talks, work experience and networking with industry professionals. This has allowed me to build tangible and transferable skills valuable in all stages of the publishing lifecycle.

I am used to working with big five publishers via their creator mailing lists to help sell their books, such as Hachette UK, HarperCollins and Bloomsbury, and currently assist within all stages of the publishing process at independent publishers, such as Ringwood and my own publication Singing Swallow. I also hold a certificate from Hachette UK for completing their online publishing work experience course over the summer of 2025. I currently love creating social media content and graphics using Canva for my book-focused TikTok account @bookedbykerry, and enjoy writing and submitting work to competitions.

Please enjoy having a scroll and explore through all my credentials and passions for the book industry and do get in touch!
